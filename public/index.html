// WHAT IS BUILD MY CLUB

Build My Club puts you in charge of a youth football academy. Every decision is yours — which players to recruit, which coaches to hire, which sponsors to take on, and how to balance the books while building a squad capable of producing world-class talent.

The game runs offline on your device. Your academy evolves week by week through training, aging, and the ever-present pressure of the transfer market. Sync to the global server to submit your score and see where you stand against every other manager in the world.
